Decimate Your Opponents With Customizable Paddles

The rear paddles are a game-changer...literally. Tucked underneath where your fingers naturally rest, they let you keep your thumbs on the sticks while still hitting important buttons. Whether you're reloading, jumping, or switching weapons, the paddles make it feel faster and more fluid. They're fully customizable too, so you can map them to whatever feels right for your playstyle. And if they’re not your thing, no worries! They pop off easily. It’s all about giving you more control without getting in the way.

Swap Your Sticks For A Competitive Edge

The sticks feel like they were made just for you. You can swap out the tops to match what feels best. Taller ones for aiming, shorter for quick movement, even domed or concave options depending on your grip. You can tweak the tension too, so whether you like loose and smooth or tight and snappy, it’s totally in your hands. It’s the kind of control that just feels right the moment you start playing.

Buttons That Keep Up With Your Every Move

The buttons just feel right. They’re nice and responsive, clicking perfectly with every press so you know your input registered. Whether you’re going for fast combos or just navigating menus, they have a solid, satisfying feel that makes playing smoother and more enjoyable. It’s like the controller is tuned to keep up with you, no matter how intense things get.
